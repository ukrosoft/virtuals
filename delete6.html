A tailor made service: determining what you need

Each business is unique. When you contact us, the first thing we do is seek to understand your business requirements. Whether you are looking for an outstanding administrative assistant, a travel planner, multilingual customer support, or a social media manager – we work with you to provide a solution that’s tailored to your requirements. If you need someone to manage your whole business or a certain process on a daily basis, we will look for an online business manager.
2

Selecting the right person for you

We screen and select the best dedicated Virtual Assistant or Online Business manager, as your single point of contact. He/she will follow up most requests personally. Behind him/her will be a whole professional team adapted to your needs. If you need more specialized help in different fields, we will look together for a customized plan in order to build your team. Before you start working with your VA or OBM, you will have a meeting with him/her to see if he/she responds to your expectations.
3

Start working together

Your dedicated online business manager or VA will take care of all your tasks and report you consistently. At the beginning of our cooperation, we provide you the right tools to maximize communication and efficiency. We'll help you get started by facilitating a launch meeting so you can orientate your assistant to your work and the tasks you want completed. You will always be able to keep track of what tasks your assistant is performing and how long it's taking in your user-friendly Client Dashboard. We recommend that you over-communicate in the beginning to set the stage, and be as available as possible to answer questions. Please refer to our new client guide for tips on how to make the most of your new partnership.
